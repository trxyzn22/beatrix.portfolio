AI Lead Optimizer (Zapier Workflow)

A multi-path AI-powered lead automation system built with Zapier that classifies, processes, and nurtures incoming leads. The system dynamically routes leads based on conditions, performs AI analysis, and triggers automated email follow-ups and CRM updates.

Overview

This system automates lead management by combining conditional routing, AI analysis, and CRM integration. It ensures each lead receives the appropriate response based on their behavior, status, and qualification level, reducing manual work while increasing engagement and conversion rates.

Challenges & Solutions

Handling Multiple Lead Scenarios

Challenge: Leads required different actions depending on status, behavior, and response timing.

Solution: Implemented Zapier Paths to route leads into multiple conditional workflows.

Automating Follow-ups Without Being Spammy

Challenge: Continuous follow-ups risked overwhelming or annoying leads.

Solution: Used delays, filters, and conditional checks to send timed and relevant messages.

Lead Qualification and Prioritization

Challenge: Identifying high-value leads required manual evaluation.

Solution: Integrated AI analysis to score and categorize leads automatically.

Architecture & System Design

Trigger (Notion)

Path Split
